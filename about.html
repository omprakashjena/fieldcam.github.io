The Standard for Field Data

FieldCam was born out of a simple need: the need for visual documentation that is indisputable. In an era of digital manipulation, we believe that professional field work deserves a professional verification layer.

Our team consists of engineers and security experts dedicated to creating a local-first, privacy-respecting platform that integrates hardware-locked metadata with cryptographic signatures.
