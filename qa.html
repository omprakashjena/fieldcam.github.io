Does FieldCam store my photos on its servers?
No. FieldCam is a local-first application. All your photos, videos, and cryptographic hashes are stored exclusively on your device. We have no access to your media unless you choose to share it.
Security • Dec 18, 2025
How does the QR verification handle WhatsApp compression?
Our verification technology uses a specialized error-correction algorithm that survives social media compression. While the image pixels might degrade, the cryptographic signature remains extractable for verification.
Hashing • Dec 15, 2025
Can I use custom company logos for the stamps?
Yes. Professional users can upload their organization's logo in the Settings menu. This logo is then permanently burned into every capture along with the other verification metadata.
