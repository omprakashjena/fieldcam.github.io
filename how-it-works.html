Streamlined
Documentation.

A precision-engineered 5-step workflow designed for field documentation experts.

STEP 01

Secure Capture

Open FieldCam and capture your evidence. The app automatically fetches hardware-synced GPS and NTP time to ensure the moment is locked accurately.

STEP 02

Stamp & Hash

Metadata is stamped visually into the photo while a unique SHA-256 cryptographic hash is generated. An encrypted QR seal is added to provide an instant verification passport.

STEP 03

Auto-Organization

Your captures are automatically sorted into structured folders based on the date and category. No more messy phone galleries; your project data is audit-ready immediately.

STEP 04

Instant Verification

Anyone with the photo can scan the embedded QR code using FieldCam or a standard scanner. The app verifies the digital fingerprint to ensure the photo hasn't been edited or tampered with.

STEP 05

Official Export

Generate professional PDF inspection reports, ZIP archives, or KML map exports. Share your verified evidence directly with stakeholders via official channels.
