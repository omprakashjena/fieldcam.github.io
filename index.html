What is FieldCam?

Designed for inspectors, engineers, surveyors, and government professionals who require indisputable visual records. FieldCam embeds metadata directly into your photos and videos, ensuring long-term authenticity.

Verified Stamping

Date, time, GPS coordinates, and accuracy details are burned directly into the image pixels.

Secure QR Code

Each photo includes an encrypted QR code containing cryptographic verification data.

Tamper Detection

Industry-standard SHA-256 hashing detects if even a single pixel has been modified.

Offline-First

Capture and verify without internet. Ideal for remote sites and high-security areas.

Pro Exports

Generate PDF reports, Map files (KML), and ZIP archives ready for official submission.

WhatsApp-Safe

Our verification technology is engineered to survive heavy social media compression.
