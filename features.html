Powerful Features for
Professionals.

Engineered to handle high-stakes field documentation with ease and absolute security.

Verified Capture

High-resolution photos and videos captured with hardware-locked metadata. Gallery imports are restricted to ensure every pixel is original.

  • • NTP/GPS synced time
  • • Precision Coordinates
  • • Altitude & Accuracy

Encrypted QR Seals

Every capture includes an encrypted QR code. Scan it to verify the photo's digital fingerprint and original location data instantly.

  • • RSA Encryption
  • • Offline Verification
  • • Compression Resistant

Advanced Gallery

A specialized ecosystem for managing thousands of field captures. Advanced search and smart filters keep you organized.

  • • Smart Folder Sorting
  • • Map & Heatmap View
  • • Metadata Search

Tamper Detection

SHA-256 hashing architecture. Our scanner re-calculates pixel data to detect even the slightest modification or re-compression.

Official Exports

Convert your field data into professional reports. Export to PDF, CSV, or KML formats ready for stakeholder submission.

Category Management

Create project-specific categories and templates. Automatically organize photos into structured directories as you snap them.
