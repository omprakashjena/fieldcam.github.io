Privacy
Committed.

We treat your data like evidence—secure, local, and entirely under your control.

Core Principles

FieldCam is designed from the ground up to be a privacy-first application. We do not sell your data, we do not track your behavior, and we do not have access to your captures.

1. Data Ownership

Every photo and video captured with FieldCam is stored locally on your device. We do not maintain any servers that store your media. Your data belongs to you.

2. Permission Usage

  • • Camera: Used only to capture photos/videos within the app.
  • • Location: Used to provide hardware-locked GPS verification stamps.
  • • Storage: Used to save and organize your files locally.
  • • Microphone: Used only for audio recording during video capture.

3. Third-Party Services

FieldCam does not include third-party analytics or behavioral trackers. If you choose to enable cloud sync (FTP / Google Drive), your data is sent only to your personal configured provider.

4. Metadata & Security

The cryptographic hashes generated by the app are stored within the image metadata themselves. We never collect these hashes or any other identification data from your device.
