Privacy Policy

Last Updated:

Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how Om Apps Studio ("we", "our", or "us") handles information in relation to the FieldCam mobile application ("App"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring transparency about our data practices.

Data Collection

FieldCam does not collect personal user data. The app is designed to operate locally on your device without transmitting personal information to external servers.

Permissions

FieldCam requests the following permissions for core app functionality:

  • Camera Permission: Used exclusively to capture photos using your device's camera.
  • Storage Permission: Used exclusively to save photos on your device's storage.

These permissions are used only for the stated purposes and nothing else. We do not access, collect, or transmit any data beyond what is necessary for the app to function.

Third-Party Services

Google AdMob

FieldCam may display advertisements using Google AdMob. If ads are displayed, Google AdMob may collect anonymous usage data according to its own privacy policy. This data collection is handled entirely by Google AdMob and is subject to Google's privacy practices.

You can learn more about Google AdMob's data practices by visiting: Google Privacy Policy

Data Sharing

No personal data is sold or shared. FieldCam does not collect personal information, and therefore, we do not sell, share, or distribute any user data to third parties.

Data Storage

All photos captured using FieldCam are stored locally on your device. We do not have access to these photos, and they are not transmitted to our servers or any third-party services.

Children's Privacy

FieldCam is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.
