Privacy Policy – FieldCam

Effective Date: 2025

1. Introduction

FieldCam is a professional field photography and documentation application designed to capture photos and videos with verified time, location, and metadata for official, legal, and professional use.

This Privacy Policy explains what data is collected, how it is used, and how it is protected. FieldCam is designed with privacy, transparency, and user control as top priorities.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Camera & Media Data

  • Photos and videos captured using the FieldCam app
  • Optional original (unstamped) images if enabled by the user

Purpose: To capture and store field documentation requested by the user.

2.2 Location Information

  • GPS latitude and longitude
  • Location accuracy
  • Altitude (if available)

Location data is collected only at the time of photo or video capture. FieldCam does not perform background or continuous location tracking.

2.3 User-Provided Information

  • Notes entered by the user
  • Category labels
  • User name or identifier (if entered)

2.4 Technical Metadata

  • Date and time of capture
  • Time source (NTP / GPS / Device)
  • Timezone at capture location
  • Cryptographic hash (SHA-256) for tamper detection

3. How We Use Your Data

  • Embed verified metadata into photos and videos
  • Generate encrypted QR codes and barcodes
  • Enable gallery, map, and verification features
  • Allow professional exports (PDF, CSV, ZIP, KML/KMZ)

FieldCam does not use your data for advertising, profiling, or analytics resale.

4. Data Storage & Retention

4.1 Local Storage

All captured photos, videos, and metadata are stored locally on the user’s device. Users retain full control over their data.

4.2 Optional Cloud Storage (User-Controlled)

  • Google Drive (user’s own Google account)
  • FTP server (user-provided credentials)
  • Email automation via user-configured Firebase Cloud Functions

FieldCam does not operate or maintain any developer-controlled server for storing user data.

5. Third-Party Services

  • Google Maps / Google Earth – for map visualization
  • Google Drive – optional cloud backup
  • Firebase – only when the user configures their own Firebase project

All third-party services are optional and governed by their respective privacy policies.

6. Data Sharing

  • FieldCam does not sell user data
  • FieldCam does not share data with advertisers
  • No data is transmitted to developer-owned servers

Data is shared only when the user explicitly shares files or enables optional sync features.

7. Security Measures

  • Encrypted QR code metadata
  • SHA-256 hash–based tamper detection
  • Read-only metadata inspection
  • No background access to camera or location

8. User Control

Users have full control over app features, permissions, cloud sync, email automation, and data deletion.

9. Children’s Privacy

FieldCam is not intended for children under the age of 13. The app does not knowingly collect personal data from children.

10. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated to reflect feature enhancements or legal requirements. Updates will be made available within the app or on the official website.
